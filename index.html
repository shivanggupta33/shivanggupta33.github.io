About Me

A dedicated Social Work and Skill Development professional with extensive experience in implementing and monitoring high-impact government initiatives. Expertise includes project management, stakeholder collaboration, skills gap analysis, and training development, with proven results in driving successful outcomes across large-scale programs.

Throughout the career, led the execution of nationally recognized schemes such as DDU-GKY, PMKVY, NRLM, NRETP, RSTP, and MMYKY—consistently surpassing targets while ensuring regulatory compliance and program integrity. Demonstrated ability to manage relationships with diverse stakeholders, including government bodies (RSLDC, UPSDM), training partners, and employers, optimizing program effectiveness and resource allocation.

Focused on identifying skill gaps and developing targeted training programs, with a track record of empowering over 6,000 women through skills-development initiatives and creating sustainable livelihood opportunities. Specialized in performance optimization, budget management, and strategic planning for large government projects. Committed to impact-driven program delivery, capacity building, and measurable community development.
